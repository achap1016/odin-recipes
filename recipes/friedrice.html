Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Cooked white rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Cook carrots and peas
  2. Add vegetable oil, garlic, and eggs
  3. Stir in cooked rice
  4. Add soy sauce and toss
  5. Drizzle with sesame oil
